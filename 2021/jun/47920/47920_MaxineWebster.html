WELCOME TO THE ALUMNI COMMUNITY

Congratulations, Class of 2021!

You have completed your degree in challenging circumstances, and we hope you are proud of what you have accomplished.

Although your time at St. Mike’s is at an end, you are now part of the impressive St. Mike’s alumni community. We encourage you to stay connected to St. Mike’s, so please ensure that we have your most regularly used email and mailing addresses (the form to submit your information is below) and connect with St. Mike’s on LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook.

A few fellow alumni wanted to send their best wishes on your achievement!



To view UofT’s spring virtual convocation ceremony, reflections and advice from the Class of 2021, and more messages from the St. Mike's community, please visit the Class of 2021 Convocation Hub here.

ALUMNI RESOURCES & BENEFITS

U of T Career services
All current UofT students and alumni who graduated within the last two years are invited to use the Career Centre services. Learn more here.


Backpack to Briefcase
Backpack to Briefcase provides opportunities for recent grads to connect with alumni and faculty in their field of study. B2B events include networking events, career skills workshops, and mentorship meals.


Next Steps Conference
Next Steps is an annual, one day conference for students and recent graduates. It is part career exploration, part preparation for life after graduation, and a great way for students and young alumni to start building their professional networks.


To find updates about Backpack to Briefcase and Next Steps Conference, click here to visit the Faculty of Arts and Science Alumni page.


LinkedIn Learning
Register for free access to LinkedIn Learning. Learn more and register here.


SHAKER
From parties to professional development to lectures, SHAKER is a premier event series for young U of T alumni. Watch your inbox for e-vites to upcoming SHAKERs. Learn more here.

Ten Thousand Coffees
Introducing Ten Thousand Coffees — the digital tool that links U of T students (or recent grads) with alumni over coffee, by phone, or online. U of T alumni have a lot in common and much to share. Click here to learn more.


Hart House Membership
Alumni memberships include access to classes and other resources. For more information, please click here.


Recreational Services
Discounted rates for alumni are available through the Athletic Centre. U of T Sport & Rec will maintain the temporary closure of all indoor and outdoor sport and recreation spaces and programs until further notice. Check back as restrictions lift. For more information, please click here.

 

Library Services
As an USMC graduate, you can use your Alumni Card to access the stacks in the Robarts Library or purchase an Alumni Reader Card (EMR Card), giving you access to the U of T Libraries and their services.

To find out more about its privileges and how to obtain an EMR Card, click here.

 

Alumni Magazine
All the USMC news and more!

Now that you have joined the alumni family, you will be receiving a bi-annual St. Michael’s Alumni Magazine.

Download a PDF of the Fall 2020 Issue.

We want to know what is going on with you, too! Please send your news (travels, new jobs, marriages, births, etc.) to smc.alumniaffairs@utoronto.ca, and we will publish it in the next issue.

 

Volunteer Opportunities
Are you interested in getting involved at St. Mikes? In addition to year-round events, St. Mike’s also has its own alumni volunteer groups: the Young Alumni Committee and the Alumni Association Board. For more information on how to join, please email smc.alumniaffairs@utoronto.ca.

As a new grad, you also automatically become a member of the University of Toronto Alumni Association (UTAA). For more information, email UTAA.Secretariat@utoronto.ca.
