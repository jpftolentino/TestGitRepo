You’re invited to kickstart your next chapter in a career exploration event designed just for you!

Registration is now open for the Next Steps Conference, an inspiring and energizing — and, this year, online! — event for Arts & Science students and recent grads as you prepare to embark on your next adventure.

Next Steps features a range of speakers, career exploration panels and networking opportunities. Panels will address life after undergrad, leveraging an Arts & Science degree, internships and transitioning smoothly into the workforce, among many other topics.

Whether you’re deciding between launching your career or returning to school, interested in research and development, or considering a foray into entrepreneurship and innovation, Next Steps has panels and workshops to meet your needs — and so much more.

Next Steps will help you explore your options, including those you didn’t even know you had!
