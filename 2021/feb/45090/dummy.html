School of Cities welcomes renowned urban planning expert

Urban planning and data science academic Karen Chapple joins the School of Cities as its new director.

Chapple comes from the University of California, Berkeley, where she is professor and chair of city and regional planning and holds the Carmel P. Friesen Chair in Urban Studies.

“I very much look forward to working with Karen as we collectively strive toward a brighter urban future,” says U of T President Meric Gertler.

Assistant professor receives 2020 Polanyi Prize in Physics

An assistant professor in the Department of Physics has been awarded the 2020 Polanyi Prize in Physics for her insights into dark matter and the nature of the universe.

The prestigious prize is awarded in honour of Ontario’s Nobel Prize winner John C. Polanyi, who won the 1986 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for his research in chemical kinetics.

Miriam Diamond’s search for dark matter — which makes up about one quarter of the universe — could revolutionize the core of modern physics and bring a better understanding of the universe.
