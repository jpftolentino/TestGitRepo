Professor emeritus leaves his stamp on Earth Sciences with field trip fund

Department of Earth Sciences Professor Emeritus Henry Halls recently sold part of his decades-old stamp collection to establish the Henry C. Halls Graduate Field Trip Fund, which will support new and stimulating field geological learning experiences for graduate students in Canada and beyond.

Halls, who started the collection as an eight-year-old some 70 years ago, is pleased to give back to U of T.

“Hopefully students will gain the excitement, knowledge, experiences and memories that I have treasured over my own life of adventure.”

Life lessons inspire alumna to create scholarship in mother’s name

Alumna Alethea Lyn is honouring the life lessons she learned from her late mother with a U of T scholarship in her name, the Gloria Lyn Memorial Fund.

“My mother used to say that, as long as you had an education, you would always be able to take care of yourself,” says Alethea.

Her mother had started school in a village post office in Jamaica and went on to earn a master of arts in English literature in 1971 from U of T, thanks to a scholarship.
