ACTUARIAL SCIENCE MENTORSHIP PROGRAM
 
 
Dear ##OptionalParameter1##,

Mentorship is the perfect way for you to play a key role in preparing the next generation of actuarial professionals and stay connected with U of T’s actuarial sciences community and the Department of Statistical Sciences. Please consider becoming a mentor by completing this online form by August 29.

You can choose to support one or more current actuarial students in both academic and/or career development and give them the benefit of your industry experience and expertise. You will be matched with your mentee(s) according to the information provided by you in the online form. Matches will be confirmed by the end of September. You and your mentee(s) will stay connected at mutually convenient times during the school year via email, video call, audio call or in person — whichever works for you.

Please review our Mentor Guide and don’t hesitate to contact U of T’s Actuarial Science Club or Mentorship Programs Coordinator Ivan Nguyen with any questions about the program.

Thank you again for considering your continued participation as a mentor in our Actuarial Science Mentorship Program run by U of T’s Actuarial Sciences Club and the Department of Statistical Sciences.
