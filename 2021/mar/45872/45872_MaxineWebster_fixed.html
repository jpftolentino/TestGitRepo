Dear Alumni and Friends,

I have been working at St. Mike’s for a few months now. Like I mentioned in my InsightOut blog post, this is an interesting time to be working in university alumni relations, as so many things have shifted in the last several months due to COVID-19.

Traditionally, St. Mike's alumni would be looking forward to various in-person social events. On the flip side, alumni who may not have been able to access a local event in the past can now tune in, a unique opportunity to connect.

So, while we all wait to return to “normal” when it is safe to do so, it is my job to engage and support the alumni community throughout this time—and I think we can all agree it is more important than ever to stay as connected to one another as we can.

Over the next few months, I look forward to onboarding new Alumni Association Board and Young Alumni Committee members, as these groups provide invaluable perspectives and contributions as the mouthpiece for St. Mike's alumni at large.

We have implemented new virtual programming which you will see below, as well as traditional programming—the Annual Twilight Lenten Retreat and Alumni Reunion—offered in new ways, for now.

Even in the midst of a challenging season, I am constantly reminded that St. Mike’s is a special place. I look forward to continuing to spend time in conversation with alumni in an effort to connect, learn, share ideas, and make plans. I always enjoy hearing from alumni—feel free to reach out if you have not already!

Maxine Webster, Advancement Officer, Alumni Engagement
University of St. Michael’s College

InsightOut Submissions
 

As Alumni Reunion approaches, we are collecting St. Mike’s stories to share on InsightOut, a blog for students, alumni, faculty, and staff that runs Mondays and Thursdays.

We would love to hear from you! What was your favourite place to study or celebrate? Did you perform at Kelly's Corner or in a St. Mike’s production? Were you involved in sports or in student government?

Send us a piece about how your time at St. Mike’s made a difference in your life. Share your memories in 750-1000 words, in a conversational tone, as you would share with classmates, accompanied by a brief biography and photo. We will be happy help with editing and spell checking.

For more information, or to talk about a possible submission, please contact Catherine Mulroney, Communications Officer, at catherine.mulroney@utoronto.ca.

Accepting Applications: Alumni Association Board
 

Do you have ideas about how to engage, celebrate, and strengthen alumni connections at St. Mike’s? Would you like to learn more about St. Mike’s plans and priorities and offer your perspectives?

The mission of the USMC Alumni Association Board is to create and nurture relationships between the University and its alumni, as well as within the alumni community. The Board also represents alumni perspectives on decisions that affect the future of St. Mike’s.

A strong Alumni Association Board makes for a stronger St. Mike’s, and we are seeking new Board members to help bring your voices to the table, champion your passions, and develop alumni programming geared to alumni like you.

For more information about Board membership, please contact Maxine Webster, Advancement Officer, at maxine.webster@utoronto.ca.

Alumni Reunion
 

Please save the date for Alumni Reunion — Home Edition, taking place May 26–30, 2021. Our biggest annual alumni gathering is going virtual, with a wide array of reimagined events and activities that will bring the excitement and camaraderie of reunion into your home.

This year, we are planning special events for the honoured alumni of 2020 and 2021: anyone who graduated in a year ending in 0, 1, 5, or 6. Of course, there will be something for everyone and all alumni are welcome to attend.

There will be events through UofT and USMC, including lectures by the University's top thinkers, virtual get-togethers, and fun activities.

Evites for all UofT events will be sent in March. Evites for all USMC events will be sent in April and May. Be sure to check your email for ongoing updates and details. For more information about Alumni Reunion — Home Edition, please contact Maxine Webster, Advancement Officer, at maxine.webster@utoronto.ca.
