St. Mike’s 180: Rooted in the Future

Your advice is helping to shape our future

Over the past year, alumni have played an important role in shaping the vision for “St. Mike’s 180 – Rooted in the Future” by joining with students, faculty, staff, collegium members and community partners to share valuable perspectives about how we can build on our historical and current strengths and ensure this community flourishes in the years ahead.

Now, as President Sylvester explains here we are sharing the emerging high-level priorities and goals that have developed through these consultations and discussions and asking you for your feedback as we finalize the plan.

Thank you to all who have provided feedback so far. Alumni engagement in this important process will help St. Michael’s ensure a vital and relevant future in service to our students and the common good. I hope you can take a moment to look through this progress report and share your thoughts and suggestions through the survey below.

If you have any questions or wish to arrange a telephone conversation to share your ideas please contact me at 416-926-7261 or kathryn.elton@utoronto.ca.

Three Strategic Priorities and Goals


Strategic Priority 1: Cultivate a Connected and Supportive Community

Foster a diverse and engaged community that has the tools to succeed on and beyond campus and tackle the world’s most important challenges with compassion.

Strategic Priority 2: Champion a Transformative Educational Experience

Provide students with a transformative educational experience that extends beyond the classroom and has lifelong value.

Strategic Priority 3: Steward a financially resilient, socially conscious and environmentally sustainable institution

Ensure a resilient and thriving institution that is led with transparency and integrity.
