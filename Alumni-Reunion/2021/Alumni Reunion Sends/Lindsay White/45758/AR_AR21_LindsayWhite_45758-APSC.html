It's time to celebrate the anniversary of your U of T Engineering graduation! Save the date for Alumni Reunion — Home Edition, taking place May 26–30, 2021, and watch out for official registration materials, which will be sent in early spring.

Until then, mark your calendars. We look forward to seeing you online soon!

This year, Reunion is celebrating all classes that end in a 0, 1, 5, or 6 (ex. 6T0, 7T1, 1T5, 9T6). Have you lost touch with your classmates? Are you hoping to connect and celebrate this year? Our office is always looking for new SkuleTM Ambassador's to help with Alumni Reunion. If you would like more information about this volunteer opportunity please visit our website.

For more information please contact Lindsay White, Advancement Events Officer at la.white@utoronto.ca or 416-978-4941. You can also learn more about Alumni Reunion at uoft.me/skulereunion.
