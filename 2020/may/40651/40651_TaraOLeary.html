Introducing the ‘I’m Pharmacy’ Podcast

The I’m Pharmacy Podcast from the University of Toronto’s Leslie Dan Faculty of Pharmacy dives deep into both emerging professional trends in pharmacy practice and breakthrough science led by our world-leading faculty and research teams. It shines a light on student experiences, interests and growth and taps into the widespread success of our alumni community.

Our first season, created and hosted by Doctor of Pharmacy students, explores hot topics in pharmacy practice and pharmacotherapy.
