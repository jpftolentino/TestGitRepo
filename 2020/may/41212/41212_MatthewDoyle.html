Virtual Events
 
Paolo Granata, Assistant Professor Book and Media Studies, was looking forward to presenting a talk about Marshall McLuhan at our Saturday lecture this weekend. During this time of physical distancing, Paolo has launched a weekly webinar series to keep our community connected. We are sharing it with you with the best wishes.
Webinar Series hosted by
Paolo Granata

Hosted by Paolo Granata, the McLuhan Institute's Monday Night Webinars bring back the spirit of Marshall McLuhan's famous Monday Night Seminars for the global village in the era of COVID19.

The Monday Night Webinars are an opportunity for an open discussion, a creative and respectful dialogue for making sense of our contemporary media landscape and for releasing the energy of new and outrageous ideas, innovative thought, and transformative understanding and action in response to the current social scenario.
