ALUMNI SURVEY
 
 
Dear ##OptionalParameter1##,

I hope you are staying well during this unusual time. The sudden move to an online format in the middle of the 2020-21 academic year has been a challenge our faculty have met with great creativity to enable students to complete their program requirements, including a very successful virtual thesis presentation event.

This change of teaching format comes in the midst of an Earth Sciences self-study, part of the University of Toronto Quality Assurance Process review process for ongoing appraisal and quality improvement of programs to ensure they meet the highest standards of academic excellence.

For this review, we are asked to contrast what we think we teach with what our students perceive we teach, and then again explore how this is different from what our Alumni think. Taking this short survey (three minutes) will help us better understand the strengths and shortcomings of our programs, and your participation is greatly appreciated.

In this process, we are asking recent Earth Sciences alumni to fill out an anonymous survey on what we teach and how we teach. I hope you can find a few minutes to take the survey and let us know how we did in preparing you for your career.

Take the Survey Now


Stay well, enjoy your summer and keep in touch! We look forward to seeing you when we are able to schedule events for alumni. Thank you!



Ulrich Wortmann
Associate Chair Undergraduate Affairs
Department of Earth Sciences
