Dear Alumni and Friends,

Welcome to the newest edition of GeoPlan, the Department of Geography & Planning newsletter. We hope everyone is staying healthy and safe during these very challenging times. With everything going on around us, we thought now would be the perfect time to reconnect with you — virtually.

For the past decade, the Chair’s Message has been delivered by Professor Virginia Maclaren. The tri-campus committee charged with selecting Virginia’s successor decided to split the position into two. On July 1, 2018, Richard DiFrancesco assumed the position of Chair of the St. George (undergraduate) Department of Geography & Planning and, on January 1, 2019, Ronald Buliung assumed the position of Graduate Chair of the Tri-Campus Graduate Department of Geography & Planning.

The fact that, to date, each of us has been kept very busy speaks to how efficient and dedicated Virginia was in both of these roles. We look forward to sharing other changes to the Geography & Planning team in the pages of this newsletter.

There is so much more to tell you about our community — about keynote addresses, mountain adventures and walking tours through Mexico City, to name just a few, as well as plenty of awards and honourable mentions.

You can read all about these Geography & Planning happenings here.

Stay safe — and keep in touch. We look forward to continuing to share our adventures, events and accomplishments in the future.

Richard DiFrancesco, Chair of the St. George (undergraduate)
Department of Geography & Planning
