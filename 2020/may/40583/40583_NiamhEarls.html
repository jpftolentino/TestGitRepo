Dear Colleagues,

I want to extend my sincere gratitude to all those who supported the Faculty this year with your thoughtful giving. If you made a gift, whether big or small, thank you. You help make the Faculty of Arts & Science what it is: an interdisciplinary network of learning and innovation across the humanities, sciences and social sciences; a supportive and collaborative community; and an internationally respected hub of education, research and public engagement.

As Arts & Science faculty and staff, you see firsthand the transformative impact your generosity can have. Scholarships and bursaries help improve access to education for exceptional students from around the world.

Read why Linda Quagraine, a longtime member of the Office of the Dean, continues to donate.

Support from donors like you helps alleviate financial stress for our students, allowing them to spend more time gaining new knowledge and acquiring the critical thinking skills they need to succeed. 		HOW YOU CAN GIVE NOW

Donor support also honours the accomplishments and contributions of colleagues who have made a difference at our Faculty, and within the broader community.

Philanthropic gifts also advance our award-winning research programs in the arts, humanities, sciences and social sciences, which fuel innovation and strengthen the position of the University of Toronto—now ranked number 18 in the world—as a scholarly powerhouse tackling the most pressing issues on the global stage.

Read why Catherine Monahan, managing editor of the Dictionary of Old English, has been giving back for more than 25 years.

Once again, thank you for your thoughtful generosity. The support of faculty and staff is a foundation of our community’s strength, which is now more important than ever. As we continue to adapt to our new academic reality, I am grateful for your commitment, resilience and flexibility.

Together, we can overcome any challenge and continue to accomplish great things.

Best wishes,

Melanie A. Woodin
Dean, Faculty of Arts & Science
