MENTORSHIP PROGRAM
 
 
Dear ##OptionalParameter1##,

Thank you for expressing interest in participating in the Computer Science Mentorship Program. While we weren’t able to run the program in spring 2020, we are delighted to announce that it will go ahead in an online format for spring 2021.

Will you join us as a mentor this year?

The commitment is the same: supporting one or more students in their understanding and exploration of the professional world to help them make the transition from university to career. You will be matched in December and will communicate with your mentee(s) from January to April 2021 by video or audio call — whichever works for you.

If you’re interested in participating as a mentor, please complete this online form by December 10.

By sharing your experience, knowledge and professionalism, you will help prepare the next generation of computer scientists and support both U of T and your industry. Your involvement is especially appreciated now, as supporting students is more important than ever.

We hope you will consider participating this year. If you have any questions about the program, please do not hesitate to contact me at dianeh@cs.toronto.edu.

Thank you for considering this initiative and supporting today's computer science students.

Warm regards,

Diane Horton
Professor, Teaching Stream
Department of Computer Science
