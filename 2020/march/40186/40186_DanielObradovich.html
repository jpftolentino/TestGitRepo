Good afternoon,

Due to the recent developments with COVID-19, and following the recommendations of our academic and institutional leadership, the University is postponing the event celebrating the 20th anniversary of the naming of the Edward S. Rogers Senior Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering and the Rogers Scholarships, previously scheduled for March 31st. Though we are greatly looking forward to celebrating this landmark donation with our community, we feel it is important to prioritize everyone's safety at this time.

Once conditions permit, we will be in touch with further information about the rescheduling of this event. In the meantime, please accept our apologies and our best wishes for your continued health and wellbeing.

Yours sincerely,

Faculty of Applied Science & Engineering
