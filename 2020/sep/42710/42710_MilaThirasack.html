ACTUARIAL SCIENCE MENTORSHIP PROGRAM
 
 
Dear ##OptionalParameter12##,

Thank you for participating in last year's Actuarial Science Mentorship Program. Will you please join us again this year?

The commitment is the same: supporting one or more students in their understanding and exploration of the professional world to help them make the transition from university to career. You will be matched during October and will communicate with your mentee(s) during the school year via email, video call or audio call — whichever works for you.

By sharing your experience, knowledge and professionalism, you will help prepare the next generation of Actuaries and support both your university and your industry. Your involvement is especially appreciated now during these unprecedented times, as student resources are very limited.

We hope you will consider participating again. If you have any questions about the program, please do not hesitate to contact the Actuarial Science Club or review the Mentor Handbook.

Thank you for considering this fantastic initiative and supporting today's Actuarial Science students.


Warm regards,

University of Toronto Actuarial Science Club
