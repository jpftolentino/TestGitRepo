We are so grateful you connected with us in 2019–20 to generously share your time for networking panels, career advice and mentorship with Arts & Science students. Thank you!

You can be proud of having truly made a difference in their lives.

Today, as we look ahead and plan for our upcoming academic year, we hope we can count on you to volunteer with us once again, virtually.

Now more than ever, in these challenging economic times, students need you to share your lived experiences, to mentor and guide them.

Alumni connections with new and returning students, from Toronto and around the world, can have a transformative impact on our community and help show students they are not alone.

Imagine the questions they have now as they prepare for the fall and will have soon when classes begin. Think about the alumni support they will wholeheartedly appreciate — and consider how you can help.

As a past mentor, remember how rewarding it was when you were able to demystify course selections and career trajectories, share your success stories and advice, and set them on exciting new paths.

By offering the wisdom of your career experiences to our students, you empower them to aim high and realize their potential. More importantly, you give them confidence and help make their decisions seem less daunting.

Will you volunteer with us again?

Please do not hesitate to contact me should you have any questions or suggestions. I’d be delighted to hear from you. Enjoy a safe and healthy summer!
