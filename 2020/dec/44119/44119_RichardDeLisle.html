For more than 50 years, the Dictionary of Canadian Biography/Dictionnaire biographique du Canada (DCB/DBC) has been telling the incredible stories of the diverse individuals from all walks of life who reflect our country’s rich, multilayered history — and it would not have been possible without the support of friends and donors like you. Thank you!

With a global shift for so many to teaching, learning and working remotely, and as Canada’s reputation continues to shine on the international stage, the need for a thorough and accessible digital compendium preserving Canada’s biographical history and accomplishments has never been more apparent. Teachers, students and lifelong learners alike need the DCB/ DBC now more than ever.

A gift to the DCB/DBC not only preserves the amazing stories that make up the Canada we know today, but also ensures this invaluable resource remains accessible tomorrow and beyond for global citizens.

As the first and only fully bilingual dictionary of national biography in the world, the DCB/DBC is a jewel of a resource for students, historians and countless others with an enduring curiosity about Canada. It’s also freely available online, attracting more than 1 million visitors every year — and that number is continuing to grow. Last year, the DCB/DBC’s pages were viewed nearly 2.8 million times! We hope to welcome more and more online visitors as we expand this project digitally.

I’m thrilled to say that, since the DCB/DBC’s inception in 1966, we have produced more than 8,800 biographies — and we’re just getting started.

That’s why we need your help.

Take a moment now to support the DCB/DBC with an online donation here. Gifts of $1,000 — just $84 a month — provide you with an exclusive opportunity to “adopt” a DCB/DBC biography, either in your own name or as a tribute to a friend or loved one. Your dedication will be featured in perpetuity on the DCB/DBC’s online honour roll, and you will receive a certificate of appreciation for your selection. No donation is too small, and we welcome your gift with gratitude.

Your thoughtful generosity, past and present, is deeply appreciated and will help to secure the DCB/DBC’s position as a vital asset in today’s digital world for generations to come.

With thanks,
