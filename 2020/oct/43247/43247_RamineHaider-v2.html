A&S ALUMNI FAMILY CONTEST

 

HALLOWEEN PUMPKIN PHOTO CONTEST

You’re invited to participate in a family-friendly pumpkin decorating competition!

Submit photos of your children’s carved or decorated pumpkin and be entered for a chance to win a Halloween-themed prize pack!

The competition is perfect for junior and middle school students and their adults. The winning pumpkin will be chosen by judges who are looking for creativity.

To take part in the competition:

  • Photos need to be submitted by midnight on Halloween: Saturday, October 31, 2020.
  • One photo submission per child and should only feature the pumpkin in frame.
  • Identify the category to which you are submitting your photo:
    • pumpkins of children in elementary/junior school
    • pumpkins of children in middle school.
  • Maximum photo size: 2MB

Winners will be selected by Monday, November 2, 2020 and notified by email.

Photos will be shared on the A&S Alumni Facebook page. Please tag your photos with #AlumniHalloween2020 on social media!

Looking forward to your creative entries!

Participation is FREE.
