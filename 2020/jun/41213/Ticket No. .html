Wrapping up the Year at St. Mike’s!

Dear Alumni and Friends,

Activities in May and June usually mark the end of the academic year and the beginning of summer for our alumni team. The last several weeks have certainly been different from how we pictured this year ending. Our plans included the campus being filled with alumni, students and families celebrating Alumni Reunion and Convocation. When COVID-19 related restrictions meant St. Mike’s had to celebrate these milestones virtually, alumni and students rose to the occasion with great resiliency. Planning is underway to celebrate these occasions in person as soon as we are able.

This year-end update video from President David Sylvester and the creation of a Convocation Hub are two of the ways we connected with and celebrated alumni and students and I am delighted to share these with you in this newsletter. You will also find announcements about our interim Dean of Theology, and acting Principal and also about new ways we hope you will stay involved and engaged with St. Mike's during this socially-distanced time.

Please do stay in touch over the summer. We hope to see you soon!

New Appointments at St. Mike’s!



President David Sylvester is pleased to announce the appointment of Dr. John McLaughlin to a two-year term as Interim Dean of the Faculty of Theology, effective July 1, 2020. Dr. McLaughlin served as the Faculty’s Interim Dean in the 2014-15 academic year prior to Dr. Ginther’s arrival.

Professor McLaughlin joined the St. Michael’s faculty in 2002 after teaching for seven years at Wheeling Jesuit University in Wheeling, West Virginia.

A professor of Old Testament/Hebrew Bible, Dr. McLaughlin earned a BA from St. Thomas University in Fredericton, NB before enrolling at the University of Toronto, where he earned an MA in Philosophy. He then studied Theology and Scripture at St. Michael’s Faculty of Theology, earning an MDiv and a PhD.

To read more about Dr. McLaughlin’s appointment please click here.


Principal Emeritus Dr. Mark McGowan will serve as the Interim Principal of the University of St. Michael’s College for the 2020-2021 academic year, President David Sylvester has announced.

St. Michael’s Principal from 2002-2011, Dr. McGowan is an historian renowned for his work on the Catholic Church in Canada and the Great Irish Famine, as well as the lasting impact that the Famine’s mass migration had on Canada.

He is cross-appointed to U of T, where he holds tenure and has served as Deputy Chair of the History department (2017-19), as Senior Academic Advisor to the Dean of Arts & Science, International (2014-17) and as Acting Vice-Provost, Students, for the University of Toronto for part of 2013.
